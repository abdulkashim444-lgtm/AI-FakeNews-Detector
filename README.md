# AI-FakeNews-Detector
🚀 VeritasAI – Advanced AI-Powered Fake News Detection Platform

VeritasAI is a cutting-edge Artificial Intelligence platform designed to combat misinformation, fake news, clickbait, propaganda, and misleading online content through advanced Natural Language Processing (NLP), Machine Learning, and Explainable AI technologies.

Built with a modern full-stack architecture, VeritasAI empowers users to verify the authenticity of news articles, analyze source credibility, detect bias, identify AI-generated content, and gain detailed insights into why a piece of information is classified as trustworthy or misleading.

The platform combines state-of-the-art Transformer models, real-time fact-checking mechanisms, intelligent credibility scoring, and interactive data visualizations to create a powerful and user-friendly fact verification ecosystem.

🎯 Mission

The mission of VeritasAI is to promote digital trust, transparency, and informed decision-making by leveraging Artificial Intelligence to identify misinformation and provide users with reliable, data-driven insights.
